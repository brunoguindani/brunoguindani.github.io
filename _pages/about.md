I am a **Software Engineer** at [Akamas](https://akamas.io). I pursue research on the optimization of Kubernetes-managed cloud infrastructure and AI workloads.

Until 2026, I was a researcher in the Software Engineering Group ([deepse](https://www.deepse.deib.polimi.it)) at Politecnico di Milano, focusing on statistical optimization methods for cloud-edge configuration and digital twin healthcare architectures.

I earned my Ph.D. degree in Information Technology in 2024 and my M.Sc. degree in Mathematical Engineering (Applied Statistics) in 2021.
